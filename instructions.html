How to Use the Roulette Predictor

Follow these steps to use the roulette predictor tool effectively:

  1. Enter the last number that was hit in the "Last Number" field.
  2. Specify the interval between spins in seconds in the "Interval Between Spins" field.
  3. Enter the time it takes for one complete rotation of the roulette wheel in seconds in the "Time for One Rotation" field.
  4. If you lost your last bet, enter the amount in the "Previous Bet" field. If not, you can leave it empty.
  5. Select the type of bet you have placed from the "Bet Type" dropdown menu.
  6. Enter the number of bets you have placed so far in the "Number of Bets Placed" field.
  7. Click the "Calculate" button to see the predicted results.

Understanding the Results

  • Predicted Number: The most likely number to come up next.
  • Next 3 Numbers: The next three likely numbers.
  • Next 5 Numbers: The next five likely numbers.
  • Predicted Dozen: The likely dozen (group of 12 numbers) to come up next.
  • Predicted Color: The likely color (Red or Black) to come up next.
  • Next Bet Amount: The recommended amount to bet next if you lost your previous bet, based on doubling your last bet amount.

Additional Features

The page includes a virtual roulette wheel that shows the predicted number's position. There is also a notification bar that appears after 10 minutes to remind you to bookmark the page for easy access in the future.

Language Selection

You can change the language of the page using the dropdown menu in the header. Currently, English and Finnish are supported.
